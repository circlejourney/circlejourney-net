About this project

I acknowledge the traditional owners of the land on which this project was created - the Turrbal and Yugara peoples - and honour their continuing connection to the land and waters. I pay my respects to elders past and present, and honour the Country on which we, colonial settlers and immigrants, live as guests.

This is a hobby project created and maintained by Amari Low (Circlejourney). While it was started in Meanjin (Brisbane), feel free to add pins from elsewhere in the world. Each new pin will be checked and verified. Currently, a review feature is in the works. You can also email concerns to cj@circlejourney.net.

What bubblers can I add?

Only add bubblers that are:

  • Open to public use and not on inaccessible private property.
  • Working.
